About Us

We don't just provide energy solutions, we ignite progress and power the dreams of tomorrow.

With our commitment to innovation and environmental responsibility, we offer diverse, high-quality energy products that empower communities and drive economic growth. Our sustainable fuels and eco-conscious initiatives are revolutionizing the energy landscape, illuminating paths to a brighter future.
We cater to diverse needs while ensuring reliability, efficiency, and a sustainable future through the servicing in:

  • Fueling
  • Selling Lubricants
  • Selling LPG's
  • Filters

Texol Energies stands at the forefront of energy innovation, where every drop of fuel signifies a step towards progress. Join us in shaping a sustainable tomorrow where possibilities are endless, and together, we'll fuel the journey of advancement and success.

We are not just a company; we are a beacon of excellence in the oil and gas industry. Since our inception in 2022, we have been redefining the standards of oil and gas marketing with a strong presence boasting over 10 stations in and around Kampala. Our journey has been driven by a passionate and experienced management team, committed to delivering the highest quality products and services to our valued customers. Our Managing Director's unwavering dedication to excellent customer service has been the driving force behind our success.
